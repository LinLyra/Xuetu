# XueTu — Trusted Offline Tutoring Matching Platform

## Overview

XueTu is a location-based tutoring matching and trust platform designed for university students and families seeking reliable offline tutoring services.

The platform focuses on solving major problems in traditional tutoring markets, including fragmented information channels, low matching efficiency, unclear pricing, and lack of trust mechanisms. By combining geographic matching, intelligent recommendation systems, transparent transactions, and long-term reputation records, XueTu aims to create a safer and more efficient tutoring experience for both tutors and parents.

The core vision of the project is:

> Make every tutoring relationship trustworthy from the very first lesson.

---

# Background

Traditional offline tutoring services in many cities still rely heavily on:

- WeChat groups
- Informal intermediaries
- Personal referrals
- Scattered social-media posts

These methods create several major problems:

- Low matching efficiency
- Poor geographic coordination
- Unclear pricing structures
- Information asymmetry
- Fraud and trust risks
- Lack of service records
- Difficulty in resolving disputes

Parents often struggle to verify tutor quality, while university students providing tutoring services face unstable demand channels and unfair commission systems.

XueTu was created to address these issues through a structured tutoring-service infrastructure.

---

# Problems We Identified

## Low Matching Efficiency

Traditional tutoring information is distributed through fragmented channels with limited geographic precision and poor filtering mechanisms.

Users spend excessive time searching for nearby tutors or suitable students.

---

## High Intermediary Costs

Many agencies charge:

- Service fees
- Information fees
- Trial lesson deposits

This creates financial pressure for both families and student tutors.

---

## Weak Trust Mechanisms

Existing systems often lack:

- Real identity verification
- Trial lesson records
- Long-term reputation systems
- Transparent evaluations

This increases risks for both tutors and parents.

---

## Lack of Service Records

Without attendance tracking or feedback systems:

- Tutoring quality becomes difficult to evaluate
- Disputes become harder to resolve
- Long-term trust cannot accumulate

---

# Our Solution

XueTu provides an integrated tutoring-service platform with four core features.

---

## Location-Based Matching

Users can:

- Discover nearby tutors
- Visualize locations on maps
- Quickly identify geographically suitable matches
- Reduce commuting inefficiencies

Map-based matching improves offline tutoring practicality and convenience.

---

## Intelligent Recommendation System

The platform uses preference-based recommendation mechanisms to improve matching quality.

Matching factors include:

- Subject specialization
- Academic background
- Location proximity
- Teaching style
- Availability
- User preferences

This creates more personalized and accurate tutor recommendations.

---

## Transparent Pricing System

Unlike traditional tutoring agencies, XueTu reduces unnecessary intermediary costs.

The platform emphasizes:

- Transparent pricing
- Direct matching
- Clear payment workflows
- Reduced hidden fees

This lowers costs for families while improving income fairness for student tutors.

---

## Trust & Reputation Infrastructure

The platform builds long-term trust through:

- Attendance records
- Trial lesson tracking
- User reviews
- Tutor ratings
- Historical service records

This creates a persistent tutoring reputation system instead of one-time anonymous transactions.
